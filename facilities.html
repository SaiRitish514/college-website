Facilities

Library

The college library is a well-stocked and resource-rich facility that provides students, faculty, and staff access to a wide array of books, journals, research papers, and digital resources across various disciplines. It is designed for both individual study and group discussions, offering a quiet and conducive environment for academic pursuits. The library is equipped with modern cataloging systems and offers services like reference assistance, online databases, and access to e-books, ensuring that students and researchers have easy access to the information they need for their studies and projects.

Seminar Halls

The college seminar hall is a spacious, modern facility designed for academic events, guest lectures, workshops, and student presentations. Equipped with advanced audio-visual technology, including projectors, sound systems, and screens, the hall provides an ideal setting for effective communication and interaction. It accommodates a large number of attendees, promoting a collaborative atmosphere for learning and knowledge sharing. The seminar hall is also used for seminars, conferences, and symposia, fostering intellectual growth and engagement within the college community.

Laboratory

The college boasts well-equipped laboratories for various disciplines, supporting hands-on learning and practical experimentation. These labs are designed to provide students with the necessary resources to explore theoretical concepts in real-world applications. Facilities include state-of-the-art equipment for engineering, computer science, physics, chemistry, and other branches, allowing students to conduct experiments, projects, and research. Regular updates and maintenance ensure that the labs remain at the forefront of technological advancements, fostering an enriching learning environment and preparing students for industry challenges.

Sports

The college offers a wide range of sports and recreational activities, encouraging students to engage in both team and individual sports. Facilities for outdoor sports such as cricket, football, basketball, and tennis are available, along with indoor games like table tennis, badminton, and chess. The college promotes physical fitness through regular sports events, competitions, and intramural tournaments. There is a sports committee that organizes training sessions, tournaments, and fitness programs, providing opportunities for students to excel in sports and maintain a balanced lifestyle alongside their academic pursuits.

Hostel

The college hostel provides a comfortable and secure living space for students, offering well-furnished rooms with essential amenities such as high-speed internet, 24/7 security, and recreational areas. The mess caters to a variety of dietary preferences, and laundry services are available. The hostel maintains a clean and peaceful environment, with rules to ensure discipline, such as designated visiting hours and restrictions on noise and substances like alcohol. Different room options, including single, double, and triple occupancy, cater to varying needs and budgets, and a detailed fee structure covers accommodation, meals, and maintenance costs.

Transporation

The college provides efficient transportation services, with a fleet of buses catering to the commuting needs of students and staff. These buses cover various routes, ensuring convenient pick-up and drop-off points from nearby areas to the campus. The transportation system is designed to offer a safe, reliable, and affordable way for students to commute, contributing to the overall convenience and accessibility of the college.
